Working with Stevens Athletics

Stevens Institute of Technology students have the opportunity to work with the Athletics Event Staff through Federal Work Study Employment

The overall mission of the Stevens Institute of Technology Department of Physical Education, Athletics and Recreation is to enhance the healthy lifestyles of all members of the Stevens community through providing programs, facilities and training that promote physical fitness, competition and life-skill development. The Athletics Event Staff aid the Athletics Department in setting up, breaking down, and game day duties for each of the hosted games played at Stevens Institute of Technology.

As a member of the staff, each worker helps ensure the quality, fairness, and accessibility of sporting events hosted at Stevens Institute of Technology is at the highest standard. Our flexible scheduling is ideal for students looking for ways to receive their Financial Work Study hours.

The Stevens Athletic Department offers a number of positions each academic semester to allow students to get around campus. Specific sports knowledge is not necessary but an interest in learning this knowledge is recommended for the positions.

Important: The Student Employment Office (SEO) is designed to assist faculty, community and all University departments with On Campus Part Time employment for undergraduate, graduate or professional students and some paperwork will be necessary before starting. Contact the Student Employment Office

Please note that workers are expected to sign up for open positions each week and be willing to commit regular hours to the job.

Available Positions:

Webcast Producer

All games run by the Stevens Athletics staff are live streamed to the public watching at home. In order to do this, a webcast producer is needed at each game in order to update the score, and create highlights for the game in real-time.

Camera Operator

All games run by the Stevens Athletics staff are live streamed to the public watching at home. In order to do this, a camera operator is needed at each game in order to follow the action within a game and highlight the most important sections of the field or court.

General Event Staff

General Event Staff is needed to setup events before they begin and to fill in necessary roles during the game. Depending on what sports and the amount of staffing on each day will determine where you are placed to work.

Field Staff

Multiple positions need to be filled on the field to keep play running smoothly and effectively. Field Staff continue to work throughout the game, assisting from the sideline, operating the clock, and/or ball running for the teams.

Gate Collection

Gate collection assist patrons entering and leaving the event. They direct patrons to restrooms, greet people, and sometimes distribute programs. They also sell and inspect tickets.

Music Operator

A music operator performs all the music and sound prompts that occur during the games. Furthermore, it is the music operators job to play the national anthem before the start of each game and play the correct entrance music for Stevens roster introductions.

Statisticians

The Stevens Athletic Department offers a number of positions each academic semester allowing students to get involved with the Athletics around campus. Specific sports knowledge is optional but an interest is recommended for the positions.
