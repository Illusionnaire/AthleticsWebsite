Working with Stevens Athletics

Stevens University Students have the opportunity to work in Athletics through Federal Work Study Employment

The overall mission of the Stevens Institute of Technology Department of Physical Education, Athletics and Recreation is to enhance the healthy lifestyles of all members of the Stevens community through providing programs, facilities and training that promote physical fitness, competition and life-skill development. The Athletics Event Staff aid the Athletics Department in setting up, breaking down, and game day duties for each of the hosted games played at Stevens Institute of Technology.

As a member of the staff, each worker helps ensure the quality, fairness, and accessibility of sporting events hosted at Stevens Institute of Technology is at the highest standard. Our flexible scheduling is ideal for students looking for ways to receive their Financial Work Study hours.

The Stevens Athletic Department offers a number of positions each academic semester allowing students to get involved with the Athletics around campus. Specific sports knowledge is optional but an interest is recommended for the positions.

Important: The Student Employment Office (SEO) is designed to assist faculty, community and all University departments with On Campus Part Time employment for Undergraduate, Graduate or Professional students and some paperwork will be necessary before starting. Contact the Student Employment Office

Please note that workers are expected to sign up for open positions each week and be willing to commit regular hours to the job.
